# Open-Launch

- **URL:** https://open-launch.com
- **Submit URL:** https://open-launch.com/projects/submit
- **Auth:** account (email / Google / GitHub)
- **Captcha:** unknown
- **Approval:** manual (curated; rolling launch slots)
- **Free:** yes (paid tier: SEO Growth Package $59 — custom SEO article + premium launch + high-authority backlink)
