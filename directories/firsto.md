## Gotchas

- "No gatekeeping" — every submission gets homepage visibility, but discovery is SEO-driven over time, not a 24hr spike like Product Hunt.
- Sign-in required before /projects/submit is accessible; selector capture must happen post-auth.
- 2,000+ projects launched at last check — mid-traffic but compounding SEO surface.
- Sells itself as a Product Hunt alternative with ~30 min submit vs. PH's multi-week prep — keep submission lightweight, no need for elaborate launch assets.
