## Gotchas

- No free tier — every listing requires payment ($247 Basic sold out / $497 Verified / custom Enterprise). Autofill engine should treat this as a paid-only destination and surface cost to user before submitting.
- All submissions go through editorial approval; rejection triggers full refund on Verified tier.
- Verified tier supports a video upload field (user provides their own video; Futurepedia doesn't produce).
- Form fields not publicly inspectable — needs login + tier purchase to map. Likely fields based on typical AI directory: name, tagline, description, url, logo_url, category (AI vertical), pricing model, video_url (Verified only).
- Reaches ~400k monthly visitors per their own copy — high-value listing but gated behind real money.
