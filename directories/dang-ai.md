# Dang.ai

- **URL:** https://dang.ai
- **Submit URL:** https://dang.ai/submit
- **Auth:** none (email-gated, no account)
- **Captcha:** unknown
- **Approval:** manual (free: 3-4 weeks; paid: ~48 business hours)
- **Free:** yes (paid tier: $29 fast-track, verified badge, faster review)

- **Ease:** 🟢 easy
- **Ease reason:** Public form, no login required, short field list

## Gotchas

- Free path REQUIRES a dofollow backlink to dang.ai placed somewhere on the submitted site BEFORE submission. They verify before listing.
- Form is unusually minimal — no description/tagline/category fields in the initial submit. They likely scrape your site for metadata after approval.
- Paid tier ($29) skips the backlink requirement and adds a verified badge.
- 85K+ monthly visitors per Dang's own claim — verify independently before counting on traffic.
